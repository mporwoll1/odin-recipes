Potatoes

Description

These potatoes are the perfect side dish!

Ingredients

  • bunch of potatoes
  • olice oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic
  • paprika

Steps

  1. Wash and cut potatoes into 1 inch cubes
  2. Toss poatoes with olive oil and seasonings
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  4. Place potaotes on baking sheet
  5. Put potatoes in oven for 25-30 minutes
