Lasagna

Description

This cheesy lasagna is worth the time and wait. It's a true taste of Italy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5oz) can canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6oz) cans tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16oz rocotta cheese
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and seasonings. Simmer, covered, for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally
  3. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes. DRain noodles and rinse with cold water.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  5. To assemble, spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  7. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving
