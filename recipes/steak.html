Steak-Recipe

Steak

Description

This is the perfect steak. Simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 steak
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Season steak with salt and pepper
  2. Preheat grill. Get it hot.
  3. Put steak on grill and don't touch for 2 minutes
  4. Flip steak for additional 2 minutes
  5. Flip for additional minute on each side
  6. Remove steak and let rest for 10 minutes
